Hike Hawaii

Pipiwai

Hiking in Hawaii is one of the most popular pastimes for visitors and locals alike. Maui's Pipiwai Trail in heavenly Hana is one of the best and most accessible views into a Hawaiian rain forest. With lush bamboo forests, towering waterfalls and epic shoreline views, the Pipiwai Trail will excite both experienced and first-time hikers.

Maui by Helicopter 

Maui by helicopter is a sight to behold. The lush rain forests of Hana, the heavenly grandeur of Haleakala Crater, the majestic West Maui Mountains and miles of beautiful beaches all add up to a visual adventure of a lifetime.

Maui Maps & Geography

Maui is separated into 5 distinct regions: West Maui, South Maui, Central Maui, Upcountry Maui and East Maui. The Hawaiian Islands are generally drier on the western, or leeward side, and wetter on the eastern, or windward side. Most Maui resorts can be found in sunny West Maui and South Maui while you can find the lush drive to Hana in East Maui. Haleakala volcano dominates the southeastern region of Maui with a crater 3,000 feet deep and 21 miles around.
