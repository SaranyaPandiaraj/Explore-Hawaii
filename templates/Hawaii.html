Aloha from Hawaii


There are six major islands to visit in Hawaii: Kauai, Oahu, Molokai, Lanai, Maui, and the island of Hawaii!
Each has its own distinct personality, adventures, activities and sights.
We invite you to explore the Islands of Aloha to find your own heavenly Hawaii experiences.

Hawaii Islands



Kauai

Scenic Coastline

The Hawaiian island of Kauai is a verdant jewel from the air.
With miles of beautiful beaches, spectacular canyons,
waterfalls and mountainous peaks, Kauai by helicopter
will take your breath away.

Maui

Makena Beach

Maui by helicopter is a sight to behold. The lush rain forests of Hana, the heavenly grandeur of Haleakala Crater, the majestic West Maui Mountains and miles of beautiful beaches all add
up to a visual adventure of a lifetime.

Oahu

Waikiki 

The Hawaiian island of Oahu is a study in contrasts. From the
air, the bustle of Honolulu and Waikiki counters the power of
the North Shore’s pounding surf and the majestic serenity of
the Koolau Mountains.



Big Island Hawaii

Hawi

The Big Island of Hawaii offers spectacular aerial views of
dramatic coastlines, geysering lava flows, verdant valleys
and lush waterfalls.

Lanai & Molokai 

MANELE BAY

Molokai and Lanai offer vastly different sceneries from the air.
Molokai’s sea cliffs are some of the most impressive in the world.
Lanai’s diversity is amazing from arid moonscapes.
